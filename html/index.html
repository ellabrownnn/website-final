Fun Fact

Take This To Your Grave is recognized as the band's first album, but it actully isn't. Their first album was released two months before and is called Evening Out With Your Girlfriend. This album isn't even acknowledged by the band, they consider it a very early, low-quality release that they were unhappy with at the time of recording, and view it as a rough draft rather than a true representation of their sound. Drummer Andry Hurley also was not involved in this album.
